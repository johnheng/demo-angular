Congratulations, Carina Chu!

Chemistry, Bachelor of Science (B.S.), Class of 2017

VCU School of Pharmacy, Class of 2021

What has Carina done these past four years?

Carina (as Khoi would put it) dominated chemistry to no end. You could always find her helping out her friends that were struggling with their work and she definitely knew her stuff, whether it was telling you the molecular weight of carbon or how to find the biosysnthesis of amino acids and porphyrins (I don't know what those are).

It wasn't just chemistry she was good at. She became educated all around: math, sociology, humanities, speech, physics, economics--she even attended an Operating Systems lecture with John once. She was involved in multiple student organizations and even worked part-time as a drug dealer (i.e. pharmacy technician). The money she made as a pharm tech was used to pay for her food, rent, gas, and gifts she'd buy for other people, so she grew to understand the value of a dollar.

Lastly, she worked her ass off. She really grew up and started handling everything herself. In the four years she has been at VCU, she's been through a lot and has changed in ways she couldn't imagine. She's ready to go out there and do big things.
